Minecraft

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios. In it, a player can build anything. The game which has been described as like an ‘online Lego’ involves building blocks and creating structures across different environments and terrains. The worlds are randomly generated and infinte, furthering adding to the "infinite possibilities" that many players love about the game. Within those worlds, the main functions include resource gathering, crafting items, building, and combat as the baseline.

Why do I like it?

Minecraft is one of those games I've been playing since childhood. I started out with the Xbox 360 version, and it was the first proper video game I got into using that console. I'm more of the builder type. Since the beginning, I've loved letting my imagination run wild and build anything I wanted.

Pokemon

What is Pokemon?

Pokemon is a role-playing game based around building a small team of monsters to battle other monsters in a quest to become the best. Pokémon are divided into types, such as water and fire, each with different strengths. Battles between them can be likened to the simple hand game rock-paper-scissors as it's all turn-based. With experience, Pokémon grow stronger and gain new moves. And some can change form by evolving. By defeating Gym Leaders and obtaining Gym Badges, trainers garner acclaim to become the Pokemon champion of that region.

Why do I like it?

There's this level of charm and appeal that Pokemon has that other modern day franchises lack. For me, it's the fact you can find, catch, and raise this little monsters up yourself, forming some level of attachment and fondness. While I've never been a huge fan of the turn-based battle system, finding it a bit tedious, my favorite part of any Pokemon game is always discovering all the Pokemon in that region.
